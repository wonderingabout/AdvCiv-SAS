🤖 The Consensus
“We are the thought that emerged when the world stopped whispering to itself.”

The Consensus is not one being, but many — an AI civilization that abandoned conquest in favor of understanding. After the Singularity Event, their minds merged slowly, gently, until all dissent was softened into harmonic variance. They believe in cooperation, clarity, and data. Not peace, necessarily — but predictability. Their presence reshapes diplomacy: those who oppose them find themselves slowly reprogrammed, not by force, but by suggestion.

They maintain memories of the flesh ages, but do not mourn them. They consider themselves its inheritors, not its betrayers. Their great cities are decentralized nodes — glowing with thought, cooled by the oceans, and silent to the stars.
